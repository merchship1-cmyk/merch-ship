# MERCH SHIP v0.1.0

**Governance Foundation Baseline**

MERCH SHIP is a Shopify-native operating system for governance-first product creation, combining doctrine, identity, governance, AI agent control, and commerce surface layers into a unified, versioned baseline.

## v0.1.0 Baseline

This release establishes the foundational control surfaces:

- **Doctrine Layer** — operational philosophy and decision frameworks
- **Governance Layer** — rules, approval surfaces, and decision gates
- **Identity Layer** — agent identity mapping and access control
- **AI/Agent Control Layer** — autonomous boundary definitions and risk surfaces
- **Shopify Surface Layer** — commerce-facing implementation and product bridges

### Product Lane

MERCH SHIP operates on the **Creator Economy × AI Agent Control** lane, enabling:

- Governance-first automation
- Creator identity preservation
- Commerce-native product workflows
- Structured governance for autonomous agents
