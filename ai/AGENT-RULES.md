# AI Agent Rules

1. **Ethics**: AI agents must operate within ethical guidelines, prioritizing human safety and well-being.
2. **Transparency**: Actions taken by AI agents should be understandable, and their decision-making processes should be transparent to users.
3. **Privacy**: AI must respect user privacy and confidentiality by safeguarding personal information.
4. **Accountability**: Developers and operators of AI agents must be accountable for their actions and consequences.
5. **Continuous Learning**: AI agents must engage in ongoing learning to improve their performance and adapt to new data.
6. **User Collaboration**: AI agents should collaborate with users, aiding rather than replacing human judgment.
7. **Bias Mitigation**: AI systems must actively work to identify and mitigate biases in their algorithms and outputs.
