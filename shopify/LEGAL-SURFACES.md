# Legal Surfaces for Shopify Integration

When integrating with Shopify, it's crucial to understand the legal surfaces that your store must comply with. These include:

## 1. Terms of Service
Your store should have comprehensive Terms of Service that outlines the rights and responsibilities of both the merchant and the customer.

## 2. Privacy Policy
A clear Privacy Policy is necessary to inform customers about how their data will be used, stored, and protected. 

## 3. Refund Policy
A transparent Refund Policy helps manage customer expectations regarding returns and exchanges.

## 4. Shipping Policy
Details about shipping times, costs, and procedures should be clearly stated to avoid confusion.

## 5. Compliance with Local Laws
Ensure that your store complies with local laws and regulations regarding e-commerce, such as consumer protection and tax obligations.

Maintaining up-to-date legal surfaces is essential for protecting your business and building customer trust.
